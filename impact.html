Future Goals & Roadmap

Our vision for creating lasting impact across Africa

1

Pilot Launch & Foundation Building

Current Phase

Establishing our core operations and building partnerships with local schools and communities. Focus on direct impact through school supply distribution and community engagement.

✅ Establish partnerships with 10+ schools
✅ Distribute supplies to 500+ children
✅ Build local volunteer network
✅ Develop sustainable funding model
2

School Partnerships Expansion

Next 6-12 Months

Scale our impact by partnering with more schools and implementing comprehensive support programs that address multiple educational barriers.

🎯 Partner with 50+ schools across Nigeria
🎯 Support 2,000+ children annually
🎯 Launch mentorship programs
🎯 Implement impact tracking systems
3

Community Expansion

Year 2

Expand our reach to multiple communities and regions, creating replicable models that can be scaled across different contexts and cultures.

🌍 Extend to 3+ Nigerian states
🌍 Support 5,000+ children
🌍 Develop community leadership programs
🌍 Establish regional coordination centers
4

Scholarship & Long-term Support

Year 3

Create comprehensive scholarship programs and long-term support systems that follow children through their educational journey from primary to secondary school.

📚 Launch scholarship programs
📚 Provide multi-year support packages
📚 Partner with universities for pathways
📚 Create alumni success tracking
5

Technology Access Initiative

Year 4

Bridge the digital divide by providing technology access and digital literacy training, ensuring African children are prepared for the future of work.

💻 Establish computer labs in schools
💻 Provide internet access solutions
💻 Develop digital literacy curriculum
💻 Create coding and STEM programs
6

Mentorship & Leadership Development

Year 5

Build comprehensive mentorship networks and leadership development programs that create the next generation of African change-makers and innovators.

👥 Launch mentorship matching platform
👥 Create leadership development curriculum
👥 Partner with African professionals globally
👥 Establish youth leadership councils
7

Pan-African Expansion

Year 6+

Scale our proven model across the African continent, creating a network of locally-led initiatives that address education challenges from Cape Town to Cairo.

🌍 Expand to 5+ African countries
🌍 Support 50,000+ children annually
🌍 Create continental education alliance
🌍 Develop policy influence capacity
