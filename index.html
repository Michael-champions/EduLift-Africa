Why EduLift Africa Started

A personal mission born from experience

"I know what it feels like to struggle for basic education—lacking school sandals, uniforms, bags, textbooks, and essential materials."

EduLift Africa was born from that experience. This initiative is my way of ensuring that children do not have to face the same challenges.

At the heart of this mission is love—the desire to serve, uplift, and create opportunities for children to learn, grow, and succeed.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our work

🎯

Our Mission

To support underprivileged children across Africa by providing essential school items such as uniforms, bags, textbooks, and sandals, helping them access education with dignity and confidence.

👁️

Our Vision

To build a future where every child, regardless of background, has access to basic educational needs and the opportunity to thrive.

Meet the Founder

The heart behind EduLift Africa

Founder • Education Advocate • Youth Leader

Michael Attah

Founder of EduLift Africa | Marketing Analyst Trainee | BYU-Pathway Student

Based in Lagos, Nigeria

Michael Attah is a passionate young African leader, technology enthusiast, and education advocate committed to helping children gain access to quality education and opportunities for a better future.

Growing up, Michael personally experienced the struggles many children in Africa face — lacking school sandals, uniforms, textbooks, bags, and basic educational materials. Those experiences shaped his deep understanding of how poverty can affect a child's confidence, learning experience, and future potential.

Rather than allowing those experiences to discourage him, they became the foundation for something bigger — EduLift Africa.

EduLift Africa was created from a deeply personal mission: to help children attend school with dignity, confidence, hope, and the educational support they deserve.

Alongside building EduLift Africa, Michael is currently studying Project Management, Information Communication & Technology (ICT), Digital Marketing, and Technical Support while also training professionally as a Marketing Analyst at Spero.

He strongly believes that education, leadership, technology, faith, and compassion can work together to transform lives and communities across Africa.

Through EduLift Africa, Michael hopes to build more than an initiative — he hopes to build a movement of hope, opportunity, and impact for children across the continent.
